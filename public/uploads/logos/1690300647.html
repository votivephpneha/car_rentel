Europcar - Car rental

Choose from a diverse range - from small cars to luxury class, vans and trucks - and enjoy the advantages of the worldwide Europcar station network.

Friendly service, new vehicles and low prices are part of the daily offer. Whether you are renting a vehicle for business or pleasure, be it a car or a van, Europcar has just the right rental car for you in SALZBURG AIRPORT.
Europcar's "green" programme: At Europcar they understand the environmental impact of car hire. That's why they are fully committed to a sustainable development programme and are proud to offer electric powered vehicles as well.
